About HA Electric Inc.

Anthony StJohn is a seasoned electrician who spent over 8 years in the NYC Electrical Union, gaining hands-on experience across residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

For the past 15+ years, he has proudly owned and operated HA Electric Inc., delivering clean, professional, and code-compliant electrical work across Long Island.

Fully licensed and insured, Anthony is committed to providing honest service, quality craftsmanship, and safe solutions for every customer.
